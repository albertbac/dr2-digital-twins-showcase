# Scenario Simulation and Digital Twin Workspace

## 1. App name
Digital Twins

## 2. One-line summary
A public-safe technical case for scenario simulation, digital twin reasoning, and structured decision support.

## 3. Primary user
Strategy or operations lead

## 4. Secondary user
Analyst or decision facilitator

## 5. Problem solved
Teams need a disciplined way to reason about uncertain scenarios without relying on static notes or ad hoc discussion.

## 6. Short workflow
A user frames a scenario, structures the context, simulates actors or states, reviews evidence, and compares scenario outcomes in a controlled workspace.

## 7. Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope covers scenario setup, comparison flows, simulation outputs, evidence panels, and dashboard patterns only.

## 8. High-level integrations
High-level only: model providers, document inputs, charting, and structured persistence.

## 9. What stays private and why
Simulation policies, ranking rules, prompt logic, provider wiring, and infrastructure details remain private because they are part of the operating product.

## 10. Confidence level
HIGH

## 11. Exposure risk
MEDIUM
